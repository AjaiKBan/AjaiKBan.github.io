The single greatest director in modern cinema.

Quentin Tarantino is the ultimate filmmaker. No person has ever created such novel stories and interesting characters.

Here are some of my favorite films by Mr. Tarantino and my thoughts on them.

Movie Pulp Fiction Reservoir Dogs
Why I Love this Movie This is the best movie ever made. This film has excellent dialogue, strong characters, and a brilliantly told story. This film has an amazing group of characters, each with distinct characteristics, but all beautifully portrayed.
Favorite Scene Vincent Vega (John Travolta) and Jules Winnfield (Samuel L. Jackson) talk about the differences between life in America and Europe. Joe Cabot (Lawrence Tierney) assigns codenames to the robbers.
