Here are some of my favorite films by Mr. Nolan and my thoughts on them.

Movie The Dark Knight Dunkirk
Why I Love this Movie This Batman movie manages to explore humanity's darkest instincts while still having an action-packed story. Heath Ledger as the Joker is the greatest acting performance in history. This movie tells one of the most riveting stories of World War II from the perspectives of the different parties involved.
Favorite Scene Batman (Christian Bale) chases the Joker (Heath Ledger) on the Batpod. An RAF pilot (Tom Hardy) destroys a Nazi bomber and saves the rescue effort.
